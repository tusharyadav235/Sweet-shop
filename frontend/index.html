Our Most Famous Sweet

Ghewar — The
Crown of Sweets

Jiya Lal Halwai is Varanasi's most beloved sweet shop, and our Ghewar is the reason. A centuries-old Rajasthani delicacy made from pure desi ghee, refined flour, and soaked in fragrant sugar syrup — our Ghewar is unlike anything you've tasted.

We craft our Ghewar in traditional cast-iron kadais using a technique perfected over 75 years. Available in three variants: Classic, Malai (cream-topped), and Mawa (khoya-stuffed) — each one a masterpiece.

Our Story

Sweetness Passed Down Through Four Generations

Founded in 1947 by Shri Jiya Lal Ji near the Dashashwamedh Ghat in Varanasi, our halwai shop began as a humble stall. Over 75 years, we have served millions of families with pure, honest mithai.

Our world-famous Ghewar has won regional awards and is shipped across India. Every sweet is made fresh daily using pure desi ghee, farm-fresh milk, and age-old recipes. No artificial colors, no preservatives.
